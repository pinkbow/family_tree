This site is dedicated to all the children, in-laws, grandchildren, nephews, nieces, and all the generations to come who have desended from the Benoit and Cadet family tree.

Message from Rameau

Dear children and in-laws, grandchildren, nephews and nieces, cousins and all memebers of both Benoit and Cadet families,

My real purpose of presenting to you this little piece of research is to help you familiarize not only with the names of your relatives, but also, and above and all, to look forward to know where in fact you come from , meaning your roots.

I hope you this study will be of great assistance to you, God willing, by preserving you from making any mistake that you may regret in the future.

Now, the time has come to get aboard, enjoy your ride and good luck!

Love,
